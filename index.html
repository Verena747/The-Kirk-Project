Our Mission

The Kirk Project is a public-interest initiative committed to preventing acts of violence through responsible investigation and assessment of potential threats. We believe that credible prevention requires vigilance, compassion, and respect for free expression.

What We Do

Investigate

We evaluate potential threats — political, school-related, or community-based — using evidence-based analysis and open-source data.

Educate

We promote awareness about digital behavior, online discourse, and the difference between expression and credible threats.

Prevent

By assessing early warning signs, we aim to stop violence before it occurs — protecting both individuals and the right to speak freely.

Why It Matters

Many incidents of violence show warning signs that were ignored or dismissed. The Kirk Project ensures that every concerning comment or action is responsibly reviewed — not censored, but understood — to protect both safety and liberty.
