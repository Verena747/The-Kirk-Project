Our Mission

The Kirk Project is dedicated to responsibly investigating and assessing potential threats of political violence, school-related violence, community violence, and targeted violence. Every threat—no matter how small or seemingly insignificant—is reviewed to ensure public safety and prevent credible threats from escalating.

Many comments online are overlooked or dismissed as impulsive reactions or jokes. Our mission is to take such statements seriously—not to police free speech, but to evaluate and understand risks properly to safeguard lives while respecting freedom of expression.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Objective and fair in every assessment.
  • Privacy: Respecting rights while protecting safety.
  • Transparency: Open methods and responsible communication.
  • Accountability: Every case handled ethically and professionally.

Dedication

This project is dedicated to the memory of Charlie Kirk — whose life reminds us of the importance of vigilance, free expression, and safeguarding communities from credible threats.
