Our Mission

The Kirk Project is dedicated to the responsible assessment and prevention of potential threats of violence — political, school-related, community-based, or targeted. We believe that every concern, no matter how minor it appears, deserves professional evaluation to ensure public safety and prevent tragedy.

Why It Matters

Many violent acts stem from unaddressed warning signs — comments online, behavioral shifts, or impulsive statements dismissed as jokes. The Kirk Project takes these seriously, combining data, empathy, and threat assessment expertise to intervene before harm occurs.

Core Principles

  • Thorough, evidence-based threat evaluation.
  • Respect for free speech while maintaining community safety.
  • Transparency, accountability, and collaboration.
  • Education for communities and institutions on threat prevention.

Dedicated to those who work tirelessly to identify, understand, and prevent acts of violence — ensuring safety, compassion, and justice for all.
