Our Mission

The Kirk Project is a public-interest initiative dedicated to preventing acts of political, school-related, or community-based violence through responsible investigation and assessment of potential threats.

What We Do

We identify, evaluate, and assess threats — ensuring that no concerning comment or action is dismissed or ignored. Through open-source analysis, education, and collaboration, we aim to prevent credible threats before they can escalate.

Why It Matters

Every warning sign deserves attention. We ensure that free expression is respected while credible threats are responsibly addressed — balancing liberty with security.
