About The Kirk Project

The Kirk Project was founded on a simple belief: that every potential threat deserves thoughtful attention before it becomes a tragedy. We exist to bridge the gap between freedom of expression and responsible threat assessment.

By leveraging behavioral science, open-source intelligence, and interdisciplinary analysis, our team helps communities identify and de-escalate concerning behavior before violence occurs.
