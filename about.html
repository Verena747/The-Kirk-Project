Our Purpose

The Kirk Project was founded on a single principle: that every potential act of violence deserves to be taken seriously — assessed with care, evidence, and integrity. We aim to bridge the gap between digital communication and real-world consequences, ensuring that credible threats are identified early and handled responsibly.

Our Approach

We do not police speech or silence voices. Instead, we advocate for a balanced approach: protecting both the right to free expression and the right of communities to live without fear. Our framework focuses on behavioral analysis, open-source intelligence, and community partnerships to intervene before violence occurs.

Dedication

This project is dedicated to the memory of Charlie Kirk — whose life reminds us of the importance of vigilance, free expression, and safeguarding communities from credible threats.
